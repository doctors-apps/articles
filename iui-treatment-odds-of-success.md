##IUI Treatment Odds Of Success

Intrauterine insemination (IUI) is a fertility treatment where sperm is directly inserted into a woman's uterus to facilitate fertilization. The success rate of IUI varies depending on various factors such as the age of the female partner, the quality of sperm, and any underlying fertility issues. On average, the chances of success per cycle of IUI range from 10% to 20%. However, multiple cycles of IUI may be required to achieve a successful pregnancy. It is best to consult with a fertility specialist to determine the most appropriate course of treatment based on individual circumstances and to discuss the potential success rates of IUI.

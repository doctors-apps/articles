## Gynecomastia or Chest Fat

Gynecomastia, also known as chest fat, is a condition where men develop excess breast tissue. Treatment can involve surgical procedures such as liposuction or mastectomy to remove the tissue and restore a flatter, more masculine chest contour. Liposuction is a minimally invasive procedure that removes excess fat, while mastectomy involves the surgical removal of breast tissue. The choice of treatment depends on the severity of the condition and the desired outcome. Patients should consult with a qualified plastic surgeon to discuss the best option for their individual case.

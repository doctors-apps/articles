## Low Cost Hair Transplant

Low cost hair transplant surgery involves the removal of hair follicles from a donor area, usually the back of the scalp, and transplanting them into areas where hair loss has occurred. While the cost of this procedure may vary depending on factors such as the clinic's location, the expertise of the surgeon, and the number of grafts needed, low cost options typically utilize different techniques such as FUE (Follicular Unit Extraction) or may offer discounts for smaller procedures.

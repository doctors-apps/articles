##Double Trigger Injection IVF Treatment

Double Trigger Injection IVF Treatment is a process used in in vitro fertilization (IVF) where two different hormones are administered to trigger the final maturation and release of eggs from the ovaries. The first trigger injection, often with a medication called hCG, initiates the final stages of egg maturation, while the second trigger injection, typically with a medication like GnRH agonist, helps improve the chances of successful egg retrieval. This approach is used in cases where the standard trigger injection alone may not be sufficient to optimize egg quality and potentially enhance the success rates of IVF procedures.
